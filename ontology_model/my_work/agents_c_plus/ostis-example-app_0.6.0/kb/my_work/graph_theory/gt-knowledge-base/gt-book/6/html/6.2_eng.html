Our interest in research Tremaux explained by the fact that this method leads directly to the classical recursive function graph traversal: visit a particular vertex, we mark its special label indicating that it had, then, in the mode of recursion, we visit all related to her peaks, which have not been labeled. This method is called the method of depth-first search (DFS - depth - firstsearch). This is one of the most important algorithms, which happen to encounter. DFS method is deceptively simple, since it is based on a familiar idea and its implementation does not cause difficulties; in fact it is a very flexible and powerful algorithm that is used to solve many difficult problems processing graphs.
